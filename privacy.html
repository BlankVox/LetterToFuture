Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how LetterToFuture collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our service to send a private letter to your future self.

What Data We Collect

  • Email address: Used to send your letter and management links.
  • Letter content: The message you write to your future self.
  • Delivery date: The date you choose for your letter to be delivered.

How We Use Your Data

  • To schedule and deliver your letter to your future self.
  • To send you secure management links for editing, rescheduling, or deleting your letters.
  • To improve our service and ensure a seamless user experience.

Security & Privacy

  • Your letter content is encrypted and stored securely.
  • Access to manage your letters is protected by secure, time-limited tokens sent only to your email.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Your Rights

  • You can edit, reschedule, or delete your letters at any time using your management link.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy, please contact Abdullah Al Tahir at contact@lettertofuture.com.
